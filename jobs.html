Receptionist

Robo Play Toys is seeking a friendly and organized individual to join our team as a Receptionist. As the first point of contact for visitors and callers, the ideal candidate will have excellent communication skills, a welcoming demeanor, and the ability to multitask efficiently. Responsibilities include managing phone calls, greeting guests, coordinating appointments, and providing administrative support. If you thrive in a fast-paced environment and are passionate about creating a positive first impression, we invite you to be a vital part of our dynamic team at Robo Play Toys.
