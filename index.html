    Innovative toys for a new Generation!

    Embark on a journey of exploration and learning with Robo Play Toys where innovation meets imagination! Our collection of cutting-edge toys is crafted to inspire the next generation, offering interactive and educational experiences that redefine playtime.

    Founded in 2020, Robo Play Toys was born out of a shared vision to revolutionize the way children engage with their toys. Founded by a team of passionate individuals who believe in the power of play, we set out to create a brand that seamlessly blends technology with the joy of childhood exploration. Our journey began with a commitment to developing innovative toys that not only entertain but also inspire young minds to dream, learn, and grow. As we continue to evolve, our dedication to providing cutting-edge, interactive experiences for children remains unwavering.

    • Award Winning Designs: Proud recipients of industry accolades, our toys have garnered recognition for their exceptional design, creativity, and educational value.
    • Child Centric Development: Every Robo Play product is meticulously crafted with a focus on child development, promoting learning, creativity, and critical thinking in an engaging and entertaining manner.
    • Sustainability: Taking responsibility for our environmental impact, we are dedicated to sustainable practices, from eco-friendly packaging to mindful material choices, contributing to a greener and healthier planet.

    What we have achieved so far

    Robo Play Toys has achieved substantial milestones, with a product lineup spanning over 50 innovative toys. Our designs have earned us 10 industry awards, and our toys have found homes in over 30 countries, impacting the lives of thousands of children worldwide

    CEO

    As the founder and CEO of RoboPlay Toys, Juan is passionate about creating innovative and educational robot toys that spark the imagination of children. With a long history in the toy engineering business, he created RoboPlay Toys to design the best toys at the lowest prices. On the weekends you can find Juan at the golf course, the pickleball court or out sailing. Juan loves a good deal, so you may see him at thrift stores around town.

    Alex Miller

    Chief Toy Engineer

    Alex is the mastermind behind the design and development of the robot toys at RoboPlay Toys. His creativity and technical expertise bring each toy to life. Alex is a proud Virginia Tech graduate and was the mascot (HokieBird) for 2 years at the football games. Alex and his wife Jenny are Virginia natives who spend their free time hiking. They both completed the Appalachian trail before settling down with the 3 schnauzers: Billy, Lilly, and Brooks. Interested in his dogs? Visit an area dog show and you may just meet up with Alex.
